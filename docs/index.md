# ZenLock

A privacy-first digital wellbeing app for iOS and Android.

ZenLock helps you stay focused by blocking distracting apps on your own schedule. Everything runs locally on your device — no accounts, no servers, no tracking.

## Features

- **Focus sessions** — Block selected apps for a chosen duration.
- **Time-based schedules** — Set daily windows when specific apps stay locked.
- **Usage limits** — Get warned (and optionally blocked) when an app's daily quota runs out.
- **Screen Time insights** — See where your time goes today, with a daily goal you set yourself.
- **Accountability nudges** — Friction prompts to slow down impulsive unlocks.

## Privacy

ZenLock is built around a simple principle: your data should never leave your device.

- No accounts, no login.
- No analytics, no telemetry, no third-party SDKs.
- No servers — there is no backend to send data to.
- App selections use Apple's opaque-token system; we never see the apps you choose to block.

Read the full [Privacy Policy](privacy.html).
